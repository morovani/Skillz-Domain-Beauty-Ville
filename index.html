About Us

Skillz Domain African Beauty View is a professional beauty and medical aesthetics brand founded by Ejuro Glory Egba, a licensed Head Stylist and licensed Medical Aesthetician based in Saint John, New Brunswick, Canada.


Our brand was created with a clear purpose: to deliver high-quality beauty and medical aesthetic services that enhance confidence, wellness, and natural beauty. At Skillz Domain African Beauty View, we combine advanced medical aesthetics with luxury beauty care, ensuring every service is performed with professionalism, safety, and attention to detail.


With extensive training and certification in both beauty and medical aesthetics, we provide a wide range of customized treatments designed to meet individual client needs. From relaxing self-care services to advanced skin and body treatments, we are committed to delivering visible results while maintaining the highest standards of hygiene and care.
